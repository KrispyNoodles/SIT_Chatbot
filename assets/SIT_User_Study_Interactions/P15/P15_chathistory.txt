What is SIT's vision?

The vision of the Singapore Institute of Technology (SIT) is to be a key university in Singapore that lives and breathes industry. SIT aims to cultivate students and alumni, known as SITizens, to be thinking tinkerers, lifelong learners, and catalysts for transformation. They are encouraged to care for the community and maintain connections with SIT and fellow SITizens.

SIT's vision encompasses several key goals:

Applied Learning: Focusing on practical and industry-relevant education.
Strong Alumni Network: Building a robust community of graduates.
Symbiotic Partnership with Industry and Community: Collaborating closely with industry partners and the community.
Applied Research & Innovation: Engaging in research that drives innovation and practical solutions.
Strategic University Partners: Forming alliances with other educational institutions.
People-oriented Culture: Fostering a supportive and dynamic organizational culture.
A Living Lab: Serving as a real-world testing ground for new ideas and technologies.

What is the course fee for EDE?

Chatbot:
The Bachelor of Engineering with Honours in Electronics and Data Engineering (EDE) at the Singapore Institute of Technology (SIT) is a four-year program requiring a total of 240 credits.

Subsidised Fee for Singapore Citizens: $173.00 per credit

Total Cost: 240 credits × $173.00 = $41,520
Non-Subsidised Fee (including GST): $661.63 per credit

Total Cost: 240 credits × $661.63 = $158,791.20
For more details, you can visit the Electronics and Data Engineering course page.
